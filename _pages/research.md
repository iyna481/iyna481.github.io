My research focuses on cloud microphysics, aerosol-cloud interactions, turbulence, and atmospheric modeling.

## Research topics

### Cloud chamber experiments

I study cloud microphysical processes in controlled laboratory cloud chamber environments, including convection and expansion chamber experiments.

### LES-SDM modeling

I use large-eddy simulation and the super-droplet method to investigate aerosol-dependent droplet activation, condensational growth, ice growth, and cloud microphysical variability.

### Marine stratocumulus clouds

I analyze observational data from marine stratocumulus cloud campaigns to understand entrainment, mixing, and vertical variations of cloud microphysical properties.

### Aerosol-cloud interactions

I am interested in how aerosol number concentration, composition, and turbulent transport influence cloud structure, precipitation processes, and cloud optical properties.
