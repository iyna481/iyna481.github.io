  <h1 style="margin: 0 0 0.8rem 0; font-size: 1.85rem; line-height: 1.25;">Cloud microphysics and super-droplet modeling</h1>
  <p style="margin: 0; font-size: 1.05rem; line-height: 1.65;">My research focuses on cloud microphysics, aerosol-cloud interactions, turbulence, and atmospheric modeling.</p>

At the University of Hyogo, I work on super-droplet modeling of cloud microphysical processes under the supervision of Prof. Shin-ichiro Shima.

## Research topics

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr; gap: 1.1rem; margin-top: 1rem;">
  <section style="padding: 1.1rem 1.25rem; border: 1px solid #d0d7de; border-radius: 12px; background: #ffffff;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0.45rem; font-size: 1.15rem;">Cloud chamber simulations</h3>
    <p style="margin: 0; line-height: 1.65;">Numerical simulations of laboratory cloud chamber environments, including convection and expansion chamber configurations.</p>
  </section>

  <section style="padding: 1.1rem 1.25rem; border: 1px solid #d0d7de; border-radius: 12px; background: #ffffff;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0.45rem; font-size: 1.15rem;">LES-SDM modeling</h3>
    <p style="margin: 0; line-height: 1.65;">Large-eddy simulation and the super-droplet method for aerosol-dependent droplet activation, condensational growth, ice growth, and cloud microphysical variability.</p>
  </section>

  <section style="padding: 1.1rem 1.25rem; border: 1px solid #d0d7de; border-radius: 12px; background: #ffffff;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0.45rem; font-size: 1.15rem;">Marine stratocumulus clouds</h3>
    <p style="margin: 0; line-height: 1.65;">Observational analysis of marine stratocumulus cloud campaigns, with a focus on entrainment, mixing, and vertical variations of cloud microphysical properties.</p>
  </section>

  <section style="padding: 1.1rem 1.25rem; border: 1px solid #d0d7de; border-radius: 12px; background: #ffffff;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0.45rem; font-size: 1.15rem;">Aerosol-cloud interactions</h3>
    <p style="margin: 0; line-height: 1.65;">Effects of aerosol number concentration, composition, and turbulent transport on cloud structure, precipitation processes, and cloud optical properties.</p>
  </section>
</div>
